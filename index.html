Choose Your Cats:

Pick different cats with unique abilities to defend your home.

Place Cats:

Strategically place cats on your front yard to stop the approaching monsters.

Collect Resources:

Gather resources (like suns or coins) to plant more cats.

Upgrade Cats:

Use collected resources to make your cats more powerful.

Activate Special Abilities:

Some cats have special abilities. Use them wisely during battles.

Survive Waves:

Defeat all the monsters in each wave to progress.

Win the Game:

Survive all waves to protect your home and win the game.

Game Over:

If monsters reach your home, you lose. Try again!
